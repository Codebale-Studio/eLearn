02. About Us

We are a generative art and design studio. One of our key offerings is to create customised e-educational content for high school and pre-university curriculums specially for Mathematics and Physics. We seek to help e-learning platforms engage their students better through real time, interactive visualisations of math/physics concepts that aid in better learning. Our content is developed using the javascript platform rather than the conventional keyframe animation technique. This gives them better Portability over other platforms, flexibility to incorporate design changes, and interactive features for the user to play around with and learn. We also specialise in adaptive branding and creation of art installations using data and code.
